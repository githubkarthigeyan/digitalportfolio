WELCOME TO S.K COLLEGE 


















The S.K College of Arts & Science by Thiru S.Karthigeyan.
 The Chairman of National Educational and Charitable Trust,Kadambur.
 This trust was established in 1984-1985 under the Chairmanship of Thiru S.Karthigeyan.
 The first educational institution originated S.K College of Arts & Science.
 The Chairman decided to start a self-financing Arts & Science College.
 The style and name of S.K College of Arts & Science(Co-Education).
 The College is being constructed in the land of 10 acres and 5.5 cents in Kadambur.
 The aim of this institution is to impart higher education to economically poor students in  the vicinity of Kadambur.
 The Government of Tamil Nadu has granted permission to start this Co-Educational  institution in the year 1997-1998.
 The Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli .
Now, our college has completed 2 decades of academic service to rural students.
